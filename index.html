Some More Background

  Most of my life up to this point has been dedicated to running my own successful painting business. I completed a year of computer science-related courses at Clackamas Community College after high school, but after about a year of college, I had an opportunity to start my own business. Through my business, I gained valuable experience in construction trades, business management, hiring and firing employees, working with clients, and everything else involved in running a small business. Currently, many years after starting in Computer Science, I'm returning to my passion for learning and working with computer programming languages. I'm happy and grateful for the transition. I feel better suited to this kind of work. For most of my life, I've been consistently involved in Brazilian jiu-jitsu, which has been an incredibly valuable and fulfilling experience. Through my involvement in the jiu-jitsu and self-defense martial arts community, I've gained a wealth of knowledge and skills that have benefited me in many aspects of my life. Coaching and competing with people from different backgrounds has helped me to develop my communication skills and gain a better understanding of different perspectives.
